Here we hope to answer any questions you may have about our products.

How to measure neck size?
Loop a measuring tape around the middle of your dog's neck making sure it is a bit TIGHT, fitted with no room and not easy to turn. Check our measurment instruction video bottom of this page. *If you do not have a tape measure, you can use a piece of string and then measure the string with a rigid ruler. All sizes in cm.
Will my collar stretch ?
After time leather will naturally soften and may stretch slightly but this will not impact on the functionality of the item.
Are your collars waterproof?
No. We finish off our leather products with a protecting leather balm which provides some temporary water resistance.
What are your products made from?
We use natural Turkish cow leather from a supplier who guarantee certification of traceability of origin.
